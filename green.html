Good questions

Before you ask —

How messy does it actually get?
Managed-messy! We bring table coverings, smocks-optional vibes, wipes and a full teardown. The fun stays on the table — your floors and our conscience stay clean.
Is this just for kids?
Not even close. Sensory sundaes shine for ages 4–12 and sensory jars for 2–8, but the charm bar and canvas parties are grown-up favorites — girls' nights, showers, team events. Tell us your crowd and we'll tune it to fit.
Can you prep it for me to host?
Yes! Choose fully hosted (we run the whole activity) or curated-to-host — we prep, style and deliver everything with easy instructions, and you lead the fun.
How much space do you need?
One or two standard tables' worth — indoors or out. We've set up in dining rooms, garages, backyards, gyms and park pavilions. We bring everything else.
How far will you travel?
We serve Wilmington and the greater Cape Fear region. A little farther out? Ask — a small travel fee usually covers it.
How do I lock in a date?
Send the booking form below and we'll confirm availability within one business day. A small deposit holds your date; the rest is due day-of.
