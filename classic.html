About Us

Creativity, delivered with a little sparkle

Salt & Scissors Creative Co. was born from a simple belief: kids light up when they get to make something with their own two hands. From our Coastal Creamery slime sundaes to charm bars and sensory kits, we handle the planning, the supplies, and the cleanup — so parents can be present, and kids can just create.
